Unit Lifecycle Management

Our comprehensive approach to unit lifecycle management ensures optimal performance at every stage, from occupied units to new lease signings.

Occupied

This phase provides a stable record of all currently leased units, offering a clear view with minimal upkeep until movement to the next phase. Any updates, such as tenant requests or lease modifications, are documented to ensure an accurate snapshot until the unit approaches its renewal date.

Renewal Process

Beginning 120 days before lease expiration, we leverage AI-driven tools to initiate early engagement with tenants. This approach accelerates initial outreach and gathers essential information well before lease expiry. By the time a team member steps in for direct negotiation, they're equipped with detailed insights from the data collection.

Pre-Lease

A unit enters the Pre-Lease phase when a non-renewal decision is confirmed. This proactive stage enables our leasing team to list the apartment, push targeted marketing, and channel inquiries, minimizing any lapse in tenancy. We coordinate showings with the current tenant and leverage high-quality media for maximum visibility.

Vacant

If pre-leasing doesn't yield a tenant by lease-end, the unit enters the Vacant phase. Here, we prioritize swift yet strategic action, analyzing market trends to position the unit competitively. Weekly team reviews assess pricing, potential renovations, and other enhancements to make the apartment attractive to new tenants.

Active Deal

When an application or good faith deposit is received, the unit moves to Active Deal status. This phase is powered by automation, ensuring smooth progression from application to lease signing. Our deal flow manager, combined with automated lease generation and quality checks, maintains high standards of accuracy and efficiency.

New Occupancy

This final phase signifies a successful new tenant relationship. The unit transitions to a fully leased status with all documentation complete and move-in coordination organized. Our system prompts property staff for a 30-day check-in to ensure tenant satisfaction, then returns the unit to the stable Occupied phase.
